Privacy Policy for GYW

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

Introduction

GYW ("we", "our", or "us") is a messaging and calling app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Important note: We aim to minimize the data we collect. However, GYW is not a replacement for Signal, and unless explicitly stated otherwise in-app, we do not claim that your message content is end-to-end encrypted.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Phone number (required) to create an account and sign in.
  • Profile information you choose to add, such as a display name and profile photo.
  • Content you send (messages and media) to other users. Depending on the feature, content may be stored to deliver messages, enable synchronization, and allow media sharing.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information such as device model, OS version, and app version (used for reliability and compatibility).
  • Diagnostics such as crash reports and error logs (to fix bugs and improve stability).
  • Push notification tokens (e.g., FCM/APNs tokens) used to deliver notifications.

Address book / contacts (optional)

If you grant contacts permission, the app may access your contacts to help you find and connect with people you know. You can use the app without granting contacts access.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our messaging and calling services
  • Enable video and audio calls
  • Allow you to share photos and videos
  • Deliver push notifications
  • Improve our services and reliability
  • Help prevent abuse and fraud

Permissions We Request

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To enable video calls and allow you to take photos/videos to share in messages
  • Microphone: To enable audio and video calls, and voice messages
  • Storage / Media: To save and access photos/videos you share
  • Contacts: To help you find and connect with other users (optional)
  • Notifications: To notify you about messages and calls

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable security measures designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

  • In transit: Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (e.g., HTTPS/TLS).
  • At rest: Some data may be stored on third-party infrastructure with security controls.
  • Device security: Some security features rely on your device settings (for example, a screen lock can be required for secure key storage on Android).

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the data type and feature.

Third-Party Services

GYW uses third-party services to operate core functionality. Depending on the platform and features you use, these may include:

  • Google Firebase (e.g., Authentication and Cloud Messaging) for sign-in and notifications.
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) for iOS push notifications.
  • WebRTC-related components to enable real-time audio/video calling.

These providers may process certain data on our behalf (such as device identifiers, push tokens, and diagnostic information) to deliver their services.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these cases:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (as described above).
  • For legal reasons if required to comply with law or valid legal process.
  • To protect safety of users, the public, or the integrity of our services.
  • With your direction (for example, when you choose to share content with other users).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
